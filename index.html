Research highlights

AGB binary evolution (2020)

The interaction of AGB binaries can be intensified by a mechanism called "Wind-Roche-lobe-overflow" and a circumbinary disk. Many observations imply that circumbinary disks exist around evolved binary systems. We find that if we take 3D radiation transfer into account, a circumbinary diskmay form due to the increased optical depth in the equatorial region. Please read this paper for details.
