About me

  Welcome to my website! My name is Zhuo Chen 陈卓. I am currently an associate research professor of the Institute for Advanced Study at Tsinghua University. I work on computational astrophysics. I have broad research interests in planetary science, stellar evoltion and high-performance computing. I am the main designer and coder of Guangqi 光启, a radiation hydrodynamic code.
